Hulk

The Hulk, also known as Bruce Banner, is a superhero with immense strength and durability. After a gamma radiation accident, scientist Bruce Banner transforms into the Hulk when he experiences extreme anger or stress. Known for his green skin and incredible power, the Hulk is both a formidable hero and a tragic figure struggling with his dual identity.

Real Name: Bruce Banner

First Appearance: The Incredible Hulk #1 (1962)

Origin

Bruce Banner was a brilliant scientist who was exposed to gamma radiation during an experiment. The radiation caused him to transform into the Hulk whenever he experiences intense anger or stress. While Banner seeks to control his transformations, the Hulk's immense strength and rage make him a powerful, yet unpredictable force.

Abilities and Powers

  • Superhuman Strength: The Hulk possesses unparalleled physical strength, increasing with his level of anger.
  • Durability: He has a high resistance to physical damage and can recover from injuries rapidly.
  • Regeneration: The Hulk can heal from wounds and injuries at an accelerated rate.
  • Gamma Radiation Immunity: His body is highly resistant to gamma radiation, which initially caused his transformation.

Key Storylines

  • Planet Hulk: The Hulk is exiled to a distant planet, where he becomes a gladiator and eventually leads a revolt.
  • World War Hulk: Upon returning to Earth, the Hulk seeks revenge on the heroes who betrayed him during his exile.
  • Infinity War/Endgame: The Hulk plays a crucial role in the fight against Thanos, especially in his Professor Hulk incarnation.

Personal Life

  • Family: Bruce Banner is the son of Brian and Rebecca Banner. His cousin, Jennifer Walters, also gains superpowers and becomes She-Hulk.
  • Love Interest: Betty Ross, a scientist and Bruce's love interest, is often involved in his struggles with the Hulk.
  • Allies: The Hulk has worked with the Avengers, the Defenders, and various other heroes throughout his career.

Fun Facts

  • First Appearance: The Incredible Hulk #1 (1962), created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby.
  • Hulk Smash!: The iconic phrase "Hulk Smash!" epitomizes his raw power and straightforward nature.
  • MCU Impact: Portrayed by Edward Norton and later by Mark Ruffalo, the Hulk has been a central character in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
