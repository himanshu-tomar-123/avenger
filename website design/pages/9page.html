Doctor Strange

Doctor Strange, also known as Stephen Strange, is a former neurosurgeon who becomes the Sorcerer Supreme, protecting Earth from mystical and magical threats. After a car accident severely damages his hands, Strange turns to the mystic arts to heal himself and ultimately assumes the role of Earth's primary magic practitioner. His expertise in sorcery and his journey through the mystical realms define his role as a key Marvel hero.

Real Name: Stephen Strange

First Appearance: Strange Tales #110 (1963)

Origin

Stephen Strange was a brilliant neurosurgeon whose career was cut short by a car accident that left his hands severely damaged. Desperate for a cure, he turns to mystical arts and is eventually trained by the Ancient One. Strange's journey transforms him into the Sorcerer Supreme, tasked with defending Earth from magical and mystical threats.

Abilities and Powers

  • Master of Magic: Doctor Strange is a skilled sorcerer with a vast range of magical abilities, including spellcasting and summoning mystical entities.
  • Flight and Teleportation: He can fly and teleport across dimensions and locations with ease.
  • Dimensional Travel: Strange can travel between different dimensions and parallel universes.
  • Expert in Mystical Artifacts: He wields powerful artifacts like the Eye of Agamotto and the Cloak of Levitation, enhancing his magical capabilities.

Key Storylines

  • Doctor Strange: The Oath: A storyline focusing on Strange's quest to find a cure for his dying friend while facing off against various mystical threats.
  • Infinity War/Endgame: Doctor Strange plays a crucial role in the battle against Thanos, utilizing his knowledge of the multiverse to aid the Avengers.
  • House of M: Strange deals with the fallout of Wanda Maximoff's reality-warping actions, navigating complex magical and mystical challenges.

Personal Life

  • Family: Stephen Strange’s backstory includes a strained relationship with his family, particularly after his accident.
  • Love Interest: His primary love interest is Christine Palmer, a fellow doctor who has a complicated relationship with Strange due to his mystical duties.
  • Allies: He has worked with various magical and superhero allies, including the Avengers, the Defenders, and mystical entities such as Wong and the Ancient One.

Fun Facts

  • First Appearance: Strange Tales #110 (1963), created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko.
  • Cloak of Levitation: Doctor Strange’s iconic cloak not only allows him to fly but also acts as a sentient being with its own personality.
  • MCU Impact: Portrayed by Benedict Cumberbatch, Doctor Strange has become a significant figure in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, particularly in "Doctor Strange," "Thor: Ragnarok," and "Avengers: Infinity War."
